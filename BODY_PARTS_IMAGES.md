## Quick Download Instructions:

1. Go to https://www.pexels.com
2. Search for each term (e.g., "human lips")
3. Click on a good photo
4. Click "Free Download" (select Medium or Large size)
5. Save the file with the exact name (e.g., `human-mouth.jpg`)
6. Move all downloaded files to:
   ```
   /Users/dawnclarke/Downloads/memzy-flashcard-app/public/
   ```
7. Refresh your browser to see the new images

---

## Recommended Image Specifications:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 400x400 pixels (or larger, will auto-resize)
- Quality: High resolution
- Style: Clean, professional, well-lit photos
- Background: Neutral or clean (not busy)
